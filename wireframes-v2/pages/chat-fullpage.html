Root Cause Analysis — Data Pipeline Delays
I found 3 compounding root causes across 9 delayed tasks:
  1. Snowflake connectivity failures (primary cause)
    The data-pipeline-agent has been timing out when connecting to Snowflake us-east-1 between 02:00–04:00 UTC since Apr 7. 14 errors in 3 days. This blocks the nightly ETL sync which is an upstream dependency for 6 downstream tasks.
  2. Connection pool exhaustion (contributing cause)
    When retries are attempted, the pool fills up (max 8 connections). This starves any concurrent jobs — causing the batch aggregation and dbt runs to queue indefinitely.
  3. Missing circuit breaker (systemic gap)
    There's no circuit breaker on the Snowflake connector. Failed jobs don't mark themselves as BLOCKED in the graph — they stay DELAYED, hiding the real cause from task owners.
